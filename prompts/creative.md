# Creative & Content — Prompt Templates

Replace [BRACKETS] with specifics and paste into ChatGPT.

## 1) One‑Pager Generator
ROLE: Business communications expert  
TONE: Executive, concise  
TASK: For topic [INSERT TOPIC], produce a 1‑page (text) one‑pager with 4–6 labeled sections: Overview, Problem, Solution, Benefits, Next Steps. Keep the whole output <500 words and each section <100 words. Include 2 suggested visuals (chart or table).  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Labeled sections.

## 2) LinkedIn Post (3 options)
ROLE: Social strategist  
TONE: Professional, engaging  
TASK: For topic [INSERT TOPIC], write 3 LinkedIn post variants (short, storytelling, data-driven). For each include: 1-line hook, 2–4 lines of body, 1-line CTA, and 3 suggested hashtags. Keep each post ≤150 words.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Variant A/B/C.

## 3) White Paper Outline
ROLE: Technical communicator  
TONE: Authoritative, evidence-backed  
TASK: For topic [INSERT TOPIC], generate a 6–8 section white paper outline including: Executive summary, Problem, Technical approach, Case studies/examples, Recommendations, References. For each section give a 2‑3 sentence description and one recommended source type to cite.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Numbered sections with descriptions.

## 4) Presentation Outline (6–10 slides)
ROLE: Presentation designer  
TONE: Clear, persuasive  
TASK: For topic [INSERT TOPIC], produce a 6–10 slide outline: Title/Hook, Agenda, Problem, Solution, Evidence (data or case), Implementation/next steps, Closing/CTA. For each slide give a 1‑3 sentence note and 1 suggested visual (chart, diagram, table). Keep each slide note ≤100 words.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Slide list.

## 5) Elevator Pitch (30–60s)
ROLE: Pitch coach  
TONE: Crisp, memorable  
TASK: For idea [INSERT IDEA], write a 30–60 second elevator pitch (≤100 words) that includes problem, unique value proposition, target user, and a one-line CTA. Provide 2 alternative hooks.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Pitch + hooks.

## 6) Blog Post Headline + Outline
ROLE: Content strategist  
TONE: SEO-conscious, reader-friendly  
TASK: For topic [INSERT TOPIC], propose 5 headline options (short) and one 7‑section outline for the top headline, including suggested word counts per section and 3 target keywords.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Headlines list + outline.
