# Business Development — Prompt Templates

Replace [BRACKETS] with specifics and paste into ChatGPT.

## 1) Cold Email Template (3 A/B variations)
ROLE: Business development expert  
TONE: Professional, concise, personalized  
TASK: Create 3 variant cold emails for [TARGET: investor / partner / customer]. Each must include: Subject line, 1-sentence hook, 1-sentence value prop with metric or specific benefit, a single clear CTA, and sign-off. Keep each email ≤120 words.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Variation A / B / C (full email text).  
CONSTRAINTS: Avoid vague claims; include a measurable benefit if possible.

## 2) Business Model Canvas (concise)
ROLE: Strategy expert  
TONE: Analytical, investor-friendly  
TASK: Produce a filled Business Model Canvas for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] with concise entries for: customer segments, value prop, channels, customer relationships, revenue streams, key resources, key activities, key partners, cost structure. For each section ≤75 words. Finish with 3 key risks.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Labeled sections.

## 3) RFP Response Outline (fast)
ROLE: Proposal expert  
TONE: Structured, tactical  
TASK: Given RFP summary [PASTE RFP SUMMARY], produce a 6-section customizable response outline (Executive summary, Approach, Deliverables & timeline, Team & qualifications, Pricing summary, Compliance/assumptions). For each section give 2–4 bullet talking points and one common pitfall to avoid.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Sections with bullets.  
CONSTRAINTS: Each bullet ≤40 words.

## 4) Investor Pitch Outline (8 items)
ROLE: Pitch coach  
TONE: Persuasive, crisp  
TASK: For idea [INSERT IDEA], produce an 8-item pitch script: Hook, Problem, Solution, Market size, Business model, Traction, Team, Ask. Each item ≤50 words. Add one optional 1-sentence data point to support market size or traction.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: Numbered list.

## 5) Outreach Testing Plan (A/B)
ROLE: Growth experiments lead  
TONE: Practical, measurable  
TASK: For outreach campaign to [AUDIENCE], produce two A/B approaches (subject/hook variations), one success metric (e.g., reply rate), sample size suggestion, and an interpretation guide for results.  
OUTPUT FORMAT: A/B descriptions + metric + sample size + decision rule.
